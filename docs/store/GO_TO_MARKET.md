# Mirror AI Go-To-Market Plan

## Positioning

Primary position:

> AI-powered relationship intelligence built from birth charts, synastry, relationship memory and timing.

Short promise:

> Not just "are we compatible?" — understand the loop this person activates in you.

Why this is safer for stores:

- It avoids deterministic fortune-telling claims.
- It emphasizes user reflection, memory and explainability.
- It makes the product feel meaningfully different from generic horoscope/tarot apps.

## Audience

Primary:

- Women 18-35
- Interested in astrology, tarot, birth charts, synastry and relationship uncertainty
- Wants emotional clarity without feeling judged
- Likely to pay for a deep relationship report, message guidance or weekly relationship summary

Secondary:

- Users who enjoy symbolic/spiritual tools as entertainment
- Users tracking mood, journals and relationship patterns
- Content creators who share astrology/tarot style insights

## Store Page Narrative

1. First screen: "relationship intelligence" and "loop" promise.

3. Trust proof: explanation cards, confidence notes, unknown birth-time caveats.
4. Monetization proof: what Plus/credits unlock before purchase.
5. Safety proof: not medical/legal/psychological advice, not certainty, user remains autonomous.

## Review Risk Checklist

Avoid:

- Claims of certainty about love, cheating, pregnancy, illness, death or future events
- "Psychic" claims that imply factual knowledge
- "Therapy" or diagnosis language
- Fake urgency or fear-based monetization
- Screenshots that show impossible promises

Use:

- Symbolic interpretation
- Personal reflection
- Relationship patterns
- Explainable references
- User choice and autonomy

## Official References

- Google Play store listing fields: app name 30 chars, short description 80 chars, full description 4000 chars.
- Google Play metadata/deceptive behavior policy: metadata and screenshots must accurately reflect app functionality and avoid misleading claims.
- Apple App Store product page: subtitle 30 chars, promotional text 170 chars.
- Apple App Review Guideline 4.3(b): fortune telling is a saturated category, so Mirror AI must lead with unique quality, memory, explainability and relationship intelligence.
