# Mirror AI Data Safety Draft

Use this as the working draft for Google Play Data Safety and App Store Privacy labels. Final answers should be reviewed once the exact production SDK list is frozen.

## Data Collection Summary

Mirror AI collects data for app functionality, personalization, account recovery, payments and safety. It does not sell personal data and does not use coffee photos for model training.

## Google Play Data Safety

### Data Types

Personal info:

- Email address: collected if email login is enabled.
- Name / nickname: optional display name and relationship nicknames.
- User IDs: Supabase user id and RevenueCat app user id.

Location:

- Approximate location: birth city/country and derived coordinates entered by the user for astrology calculations. The app does not need continuous device location.

Photos and videos:

- User-selected coffee cup photo: sent for one-time AI vision analysis.
- Storage policy: not permanently stored by Mirror AI. The reading stores symbolic results, not the original image.

App activity:

- Readings generated in the app.
- Relationship journal entries.
- Feedback on readings.
- AI usage logs and estimated cost telemetry.

App info and performance:

- Basic diagnostics may be generated by platform tooling and backend logs.

Financial info:

- Purchase history / subscription status through Google Play, App Store and RevenueCat.
- Mirror AI does not store card numbers.

### Purposes

- App functionality
- Personalization
- Account management
- Payment/subscription management
- Fraud, abuse and cost-control prevention
- Developer operations and debugging

### Sharing

Data may be processed by service providers that run core product functionality:

- Supabase: auth, database, Edge Functions, storage/logs
- RevenueCat: purchase and entitlement management
- Google Gemini API: AI text/vision generation
- Astrology service host: birth chart calculation service
- Google Play / Apple App Store: purchases and distribution

Do not mark data as sold. Do not mark ad tracking unless a future advertising SDK is added.

### Security

- Data encrypted in transit: yes
- User can request/delete data: yes, in-app delete flow exists
- Account deletion: yes, in-app

## App Store Privacy Labels

Likely categories:

- Contact Info: email address, if email login is enabled
- User Content: relationship journal, questions, readings, optional coffee photo for processing
- Identifiers: user id, purchase/customer id
- Purchases: subscription and credit purchase state
- Usage Data: app interactions, reading usage and AI usage telemetry
- Diagnostics: logs/errors if enabled by production tooling

Tracking:

- No cross-app tracking unless an ad/analytics SDK with tracking is added later.

Data linked to user:

- Birth details
- Relationship entries
- Readings
- Feedback
- Purchase entitlement state
- AI usage logs

Data not retained as original media:

- Coffee image content is sent for analysis and should not be kept as an original file.

## Claims To Keep Consistent

Store listing, privacy policy and in-app copy should all say:

- Coffee photos are not permanently stored by Mirror AI.
- Readings are symbolic/personal-reflection tools, not certainty or professional advice.
- Payments are handled by app stores and RevenueCat.
- Users can delete their Mirror AI data in the app.
