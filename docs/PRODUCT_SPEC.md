# Mirror AI Product Spec

Mirror AI is a premium, dark-mode-first spiritual insight assistant. It combines tarot, coffee readings, astrology-inspired inputs, numerology, relationship reflection, and personal memory.

The main product difference is not generic fortune telling. Mirror AI remembers the user's profile, feedback, relationship patterns, reading history, and preferred interpretation style so future readings become more personal and explainable.

## MVP Scope

- Auth and onboarding
- Birth information
- Deep profile quiz
- Mystic personality profile
- Daily insight
- Coffee reading flow with image upload placeholder
- Tarot reading flow
- Relationship energy analysis
- Reading history
- Feedback capture
- Memory event creation
- Credits and premium placeholders

## Positioning

Mirror AI is for entertainment, reflection, and personal awareness. It does not claim certainty about the future and does not provide medical, legal, financial, or psychological diagnosis.

## Core Promise

Not the same generic reading every day. Readings that remember context and explain what they are based on.
